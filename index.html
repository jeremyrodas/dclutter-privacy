Privacy Policy

Your photos never leave your device. dClutter operates entirely on-device using Apple's PhotoKit framework. We do not collect, upload, transmit, or store any of your photos, videos, or personal data on any server — ever.

What dClutter Does

dClutter is a storage management app for iPhone. It helps you reclaim storage space by identifying duplicate photos, large videos, screenshots, and blurry images — all processed locally on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your photos, videos, or any media files
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not collect your location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising networks or ad identifiers

Photo Library Access

dClutter requests access to your photo library solely to scan for duplicates, large files, screenshots, and blurry images. All processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's PhotoKit framework.

You can revoke photo library access at any time in Settings → dClutter → Photos.

Purchases & Subscriptions

dClutter offers optional in-app purchases (monthly, annual, and lifetime plans) processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or handle your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment data.

Purchase history is stored locally on your device via StoreKit and is used only to determine your premium status within the app.

Data Storage

dClutter stores only minimal preferences locally on your device such as app settings. No data is stored on external servers. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

dClutter does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no social features, accounts, or communication tools.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of dClutter after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
